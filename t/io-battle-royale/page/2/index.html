Tips for these extra picks

Some of the games on this page lean a little more arcade or experimental than the ones in the main list. Try them if you want unusual movement, special weapons, or lighter system requirements.

If you are on a phone or tablet, scroll through the grid and look for the “Mobile OK” chip before you open a game. On desktop, most titles work best with keyboard and mouse, though kart and tank games often feel great with a controller.
