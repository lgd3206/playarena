FAQ

Is it free and safe to play?

Yes. All games run in secure iframes from their official hosts (e.g., CrazyGames) and are free to play in your browser.

Which devices are supported?

Desktop and mobile browsers. Many games support touch or keyboard; look for mobile-friendly badges on the game card, and check the notes we add about controls.

What gets updated?

We refresh IO battle royale and ragdoll picks over time, focusing on games that still have active player bases and stable performance. When a title no longer works or disappears, we swap in a better alternative.

How is BrowserBrawl different from going directly to a game host?

Instead of listing every possible title, BrowserBrawl focuses on a narrower set of competitive and physics-focused games. We highlight input requirements, quick-match modes, and mobile-friendliness so you spend less time searching and more time playing.
